About AB Analytics

AB Analytics is a startup dedicated to delivering cost-effective, AI-powered healthcare solutions for developing countries. Our mission is to transform healthcare delivery by addressing critical challenges in medical information management.

The Challenge

In Ethiopia, preventable deaths are common due to inadequate information tracking. Hospitals rely on paper-based records, forcing doctors to make decisions based on incomplete, real-time data, while historical patient information is often missed.

Our Vision

We believe in a robust digital healthcare infrastructure powered by data and artificial intelligence to enhance patient care and operational efficiency.

Healthcare Record Centralisation

Develop a comprehensive, centralised healthcare record system to store and manage patient data efficiently and securely.

Health Information Exchange

Enable secure and efficient sharing of patient health information across different healthcare organisations.

Data-Driven Insights

Utilise collected healthcare data to drive strategic decision-making and unlock valuable healthcare insights.

AI-Powered Clinical Processes

Implement AI to automate clinical workflows and support more accurate, timely patient care decisions.
